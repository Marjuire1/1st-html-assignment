Basic Information

My name is Marjuire Nwabueze. I am 22 years old, I live in Lagos, Nigeria. I am an undergraduate of the University of Lagos, Faculty of Law and I am also an aspiring Software Enginner.

Biography

I have always had an interest in Software Engineering. I previously ventured into bootcamps like Shecodes, a coding platform to enable young girls and women thrive in Frontend Engineering. I got a scholarship but I was not able to carry on with the program due to certain technical and unforeseen difficulties but that did not stop my interest in coding. I received online lessons from play store applications like Coursera and practiced during my free time from the University. I heard about ALT School Africa from my elder sister who is also involved in STEM. It piqued my interest and I decided to apply for the KIKI MORDI Scholarship amongst others. I got a 50% scholarship offer after scoring 90% in the entrance examination. I attended all the onboarding online meetings which gave me a wonderful insight to what my ALT School experience would be and I would love to give it my best. My goal for this coding course would be to learn everything I can possibly learn to enhance my understanding of web development and make money in the future ofcourse.
